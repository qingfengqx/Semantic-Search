## 📖 What is Semantic-Search?

Semantic-Search is an application that helps you find images based on their meaning or visual similarity. You can search by typing what you want to find, or by giving an example image. The app uses a powerful model from Alibaba, called Qwen3-VL, to understand both language and images. This makes it easier to search large collections of pictures without needing complicated tools.

Built for ComfyUI, it uses a two-stage system that brings fast and accurate search results. Whether you have a few photos or thousands, Semantic-Search can help you locate images that match your queries or look like your sample images.

## 🛠️ Using Semantic-Search

The app works in two main ways: by text or by image.

### Search by Text

- In the search box at the top, type a description of the image you want to find.  
- For example, you could write “sunset over mountains” or “cat wearing a hat.”  
- Press Enter or click the search button.  
- The app will show a list of pictures that best match your description.

### Search by Image

- Click the “Search by Image” button or drag an image into the app window.  
- The app looks for pictures similar in style, color, or subject to the image you gave.  
- Results will appear below for you to browse.

### Viewing Results

- Scroll through the images the app found.  
- Click any image to see it larger.  
- You can save or export images you like for your projects.

### Additional Features

- **Filter results:** Sort images by date, similarity score, or other options.  
- **Batch processing:** Upload multiple images to find related pictures at once.  
- **Save searches:** Keep favorite queries for quick access later.

## 🔧 How It Works Behind the Scenes

Semantic-Search uses a method called a "two-stage multimodal retrieval pipeline." This means it first narrows down a large collection of images using quick checks, then deeply compares the best matches using language and visual information. 

The app relies on the Qwen3-VL model from Alibaba. This model understands both pictures and text. It converts them into numbers the computer can compare, so it can find images that match your words or sample photos even if they are quite different on the surface.

You don't need to know this process to use the app, but it explains why searches are both fast and accurate.
